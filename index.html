The Science

How Does a Levitating Moon Lamp Work?

Levitating moon lamps use magnetic levitation — the same electromagnetic principles that power maglev trains. A powerful magnet in the base creates a precisely balanced electromagnetic field. The moon-shaped lamp contains a matching magnet that locks into a stable "float" position, suspended inches above the base.

Wireless induction technology charges the LED inside the moon through the air, so there are no wires or contacts visible — just a glowing moon floating in space.
